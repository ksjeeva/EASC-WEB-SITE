Computer Science Lab I

This is the first lab, and it boasts nearly fifty high-quality computers, each equipped with the best software available. All programming languages are installed in our system, making it very useful for our students. Additionally, the lab is equipped with projector facilities, which are useful for all students.

Computer Science Lab II

This is the second lab, characterized by its well-structured computers, all of which are in good condition. The system is equipped with all software necessary for access, making it very useful and helpful for all students.

Additionally, the lab is outfitted with air conditioning and features all the necessary amenities, ensuring a comfortable and conducive learning environment for students.
